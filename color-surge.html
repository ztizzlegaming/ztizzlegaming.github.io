Privacy Policy for Color Surge

Last updated: January 1 2026

Color Surge is a free mobile game developed by Jordan Turley. Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Color Surge does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app does not require users to create an account, and no personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, location, or device identifiers) is collected by the developer.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Color Surge uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected by Google, not by the developer of Color Surge, and is governed by Google’s own Privacy Policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage

All game progress, settings, and in-game data are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

4. Children’s Privacy

Color Surge does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for general audiences.

5. Third-Party Services

Aside from Google AdMob for advertising, Color Surge does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
